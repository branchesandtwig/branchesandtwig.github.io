branches and twig

hi it’s twig :3 (aka erin)

welcome to my world... my twisted mind... >:)

here you’ll find my scattered thoughts, art projects and ideas, music i enjoy, and anything else in my life i get the urge to document.

why twig? well, i’ve been using the pseudonym for over 5 years now! i remember learning about mark twain and how he had many pen names for different satirical reasons. i thought the idea was so silly and i wanted one ever since. i have a fascination with trees and i’m shaped like a twig so it just felt fitting!
